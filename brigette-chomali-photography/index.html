Meet your photographer

Brigette Chomali

There is a fleeting magic to the earliest days of life — the curl of a newborn's fingers, the drowsy weight of a sleeping baby, the wide-eyed wonder of a toddler discovering the world for the very first time. These moments pass in the blink of an eye, and Brigette Chomali has dedicated her career to making sure they never have to.

"I don't just take photographs — I preserve the things that matter most, so families can hold onto them forever."

Based in New York, Brigette is a portrait photographer specializing in newborn and toddler photography. With a gentle, unhurried approach and a deep love for her craft, she creates a studio experience that feels less like a photoshoot and more like a warm afternoon with a trusted friend. Her sessions are thoughtfully paced around little ones — because she knows that the most genuine, breathtaking images happen when everyone feels completely at ease.

Brigette's journey into photography began with a simple but powerful realization: that a single image has the ability to stop time. What started as a personal passion quickly grew into a calling. Today, she brings that same heartfelt intention to every family who walks through her studio doors — infusing each session with patience, artistry, and an unwavering commitment to capturing authentic connection.

When she's not behind the lens, Brigette is a mother herself — and it is that perspective, more than anything else, that shapes the tenderness and care she brings to every photograph she creates.
